About Me

I'm a hard-working, quick-learning web designer with a background in Operations and Management. With my 10+ years of experience in logistics and project management I've developed a well-rounded skillset in terms of working with data, overseeing projects, both IT and Non-IT, and leading a team to great efficiency. My programming background first started with SQL and Python, from a practical sense, as I taught myself to better understand data and its structure. From there I found a love for web design and decided to pursue a career in that realm. What I lack in experience I make up for in work ethic and eagerness to learn.

My web-dev skillset is constantly changing and improving, but right now, as you can see from my included projects - the bulk of my work is done in HTML and CSS. I've had experience with JS in the past but it's been limited, so I'm diving back into that arena and working on libraries as well, so hopefully you'll soon find in my projects implementations of Angular, React, Bootstrap, etc.
